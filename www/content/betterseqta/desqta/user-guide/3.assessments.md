## Assessments Overview

The Assessments page is your central hub for all assignment-related information. It provides multiple views and powerful filtering options to help you stay on top of your academic workload.

## Accessing Assessments

Navigate to Assessments from:
- **Sidebar Menu**: Click "Assessments" in the navigation sidebar
- **Dashboard**: Click "Upcoming Assessments" widget
- **Direct URL**: `/assessments`

## Assessment Views

DesQTA offers four different views for your assessments:

### List View (Default)

A traditional list format showing all assessments in chronological order.

**Features:**
- Sortable columns (Due Date, Subject, Status)
- Expandable rows for details
- Quick action buttons
- Color-coded by subject

**Best For:**
- Quick scanning of all assessments
- Finding specific assessments
- Viewing detailed information

### Board View (Kanban)

A visual board layout organized by status (To Do, In Progress, Completed, Overdue).

**Features:**
- Drag-and-drop between columns
- Visual progress tracking
- Quick status updates
- Subject color indicators

**Best For:**
- Visual learners
- Project management style tracking
- Quick status updates

### Calendar View

A calendar layout showing assessments on their due dates.

**Features:**
- Monthly calendar view
- Day/week/month navigation
- Color-coded by subject
- Click dates to see assessments

**Best For:**
- Planning ahead
- Seeing due dates in context
- Time management

### Gantt View

A timeline view showing assessment duration and dependencies.

**Features:**
- Timeline visualization
- Duration bars
- Overlapping assessment view
- Zoom controls

**Best For:**
- Long-term planning
- Seeing assessment overlap
- Project timeline visualization

## Switching Views

Use the view selector buttons at the top of the page:
- **List**: Traditional list format
- **Board**: Kanban board layout
- **Calendar**: Calendar view
- **Gantt**: Timeline view

Your preferred view is saved automatically.

## Filtering Assessments

### Subject Filter

Filter assessments by subject:

1. Click the subject filter dropdown
2. Select subjects to include (or "All Subjects")
3. Assessments update automatically

**Multiple Selection:**
- Hold Ctrl/Cmd to select multiple subjects
- Click "Select All" to include all subjects
- Click "Clear" to reset

### Year Filter

Filter assessments by academic year:

1. Click the year selector
2. Choose a year from the dropdown
3. Only assessments from that year are shown

**Available Years:**
- Current year (default)
- Previous years
- Future years (if any)

### Status Filter

Filter by assessment status:
- **All**: Show all assessments
- **Upcoming**: Not yet due
- **Due Soon**: Due within 7 days
- **Overdue**: Past due date
- **Completed**: Finished assessments
- **Graded**: Assessments with grades

### Group By Options

Organize assessments by:
- **Subject**: Group by subject
- **Month**: Group by due date month
- **Status**: Group by completion status

## Assessment Details

Click any assessment to view full details:

### Basic Information
- **Title**: Assessment name
- **Subject**: Course/subject
- **Due Date**: When it's due
- **Status**: Current status
- **Code**: Assessment code

### Detailed Information
- **Description**: Full assessment description
- **Instructions**: How to complete it
- **Attachments**: Files and resources
- **Rubric**: Grading criteria (if available)
- **Expectations**: Learning expectations
- **Reflections**: Reflection prompts

### Grade Information
- **Final Grade**: Your grade (if graded)
- **Feedback**: Teacher feedback
- **Grade Breakdown**: Component scores

## Assessment Actions

### Mark as Complete

1. Find the assessment
2. Click the checkbox or "Mark Complete" button
3. Assessment moves to completed status

### View Submission

1. Click "View Submission" button
2. See what you've submitted
3. View submission date and time

### Download Attachments

1. Open assessment details
2. Click attachment name
3. File downloads automatically

### Add to Calendar

Export assessment due dates to your calendar:

1. Click "Add to Calendar" button
2. Choose calendar format (ICS)
3. Import into your calendar app

## Grade Predictions

DesQTA includes AI-powered grade predictions (if enabled):

### Viewing Predictions

1. Navigate to Assessments page
2. Look for "Grade Predictions" widget
3. See predicted grades based on:
   - Historical performance
   - Current grades
   - Assessment difficulty

### Prediction Factors

Predictions consider:
- Your past performance in the subject
- Current course grades
- Assessment type and weight
- Class average (if available)

**Note:** Predictions are estimates only and should not replace actual study effort.

## Tips for Managing Assessments

### Stay Organized
- Check assessments daily
- Use the calendar view for planning
- Set up reminders for important assessments

### Use Filters
- Filter by subject when focusing on specific courses
- Use status filters to prioritize work
- Group by month for long-term planning

### Track Progress
- Use board view to visualize progress
- Mark assessments complete as you finish them
- Review overdue assessments regularly

### Plan Ahead
- Use calendar view to see upcoming weeks
- Export due dates to your calendar
- Use Gantt view for complex planning

## Troubleshooting

### Missing Assessments
- Check subject filters aren't hiding them
- Verify year filter is correct
- Refresh the page
- Check SEQTA directly to confirm

### Incorrect Due Dates
- Due dates come from SEQTA
- Contact teacher if date seems wrong
- Refresh to get latest data

### Grades Not Showing
- Grades appear after teacher marks assessment
- Check if assessment is marked as graded
- Verify SEQTA permissions

### View Not Saving
- Check browser/local storage permissions
- Clear cache and try again
- Check for app updates
