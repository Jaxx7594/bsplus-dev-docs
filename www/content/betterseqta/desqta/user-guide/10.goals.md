## Goals Overview

The Goals feature allows you to set, track, and manage your academic and personal goals throughout the school year. It helps you stay focused on what you want to achieve and monitor your progress over time.

## Accessing Goals

Navigate to Goals from:
- **Sidebar Menu**: Click "Goals" in the navigation sidebar
- **Direct URL**: `/goals`

**Note:** Goals must be enabled by your school administrator. If you don't see the Goals option or get an error message, contact your school's IT department.

## Goals by Year

Goals are organized by academic year:

### Selecting a Year

1. Navigate to Goals page
2. Select a year from the dropdown
3. View goals for that year
4. Switch between years as needed

### Available Years

- **Current Year**: Your active academic year
- **Previous Years**: Past years' goals
- **Future Years**: Upcoming years (if available)

## Creating Goals

### New Goal

1. Click **"New Goal"** or **"+"** button
2. Enter goal details:
   - **Title**: What you want to achieve
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Category**: Academic, Personal, etc.
   - **Target Date**: When you want to achieve it
   - **Priority**: High, Medium, or Low

### Goal Types

Goals can be:
- **Academic**: Related to your studies
- **Personal**: Personal development goals
- **Subject-Specific**: Tied to a particular subject
- **Long-term**: Year-long objectives
- **Short-term**: Weekly or monthly goals

## Managing Goals

### Viewing Goals

- **List View**: All goals in a list
- **Category View**: Grouped by type
- **Timeline View**: Organized by date
- **Progress View**: Sorted by completion

### Editing Goals

1. Click on a goal
2. Modify any details
3. Update progress
4. Save changes

### Updating Progress

1. Open a goal
2. Update progress percentage
3. Add notes about progress
4. Mark milestones achieved

### Completing Goals

1. Open the goal
2. Click **"Mark Complete"**
3. Add completion notes
4. Goal moves to completed section

## Goal Tracking

### Progress Indicators

- **Percentage**: Visual progress bar
- **Milestones**: Key achievement points
- **Timeline**: Progress over time
- **Status**: Active, In Progress, Completed

### Progress Updates

- **Regular Check-ins**: Update weekly or monthly
- **Milestone Tracking**: Mark key achievements
- **Reflection**: Add notes about progress
- **Adjustments**: Modify goals as needed

- **Break Down**: Large goals into steps

- **Adjust**: Modify if needed

## Troubleshooting

### Goals Not Available

**Possible causes:**
- Feature not enabled by school
- Account permissions
- School configuration

**Solutions:**
- Contact school administrator
- Check with IT department
- Verify account settings

### Cannot Create Goals

**Check:**
- Goals feature is enabled
- You have creation permissions
- Year is selected correctly

**Solutions:**
- Verify feature is enabled
- Check account permissions
- Select appropriate year
- Contact support

### Goals Not Saving

**Possible causes:**
- Network issues
- Storage problems
- Browser issues

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try different browser
- Clear cache

### Progress Not Updating

**Check:**
- Changes are saved
- Page is refreshed
- Year is correct

**Solutions:**
- Save changes explicitly
- Refresh the page
- Verify year selection
- Try again
