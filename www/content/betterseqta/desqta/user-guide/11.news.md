## News Overview

The News page provides access to news feeds from various countries and regions, helping you stay informed about current events and world news directly within DesQTA.

## Accessing News

Navigate to News from:
- **Sidebar Menu**: Click "News" in the navigation sidebar
- **Direct URL**: `/news`

## News Sources

DesQTA aggregates news from multiple sources organized by region:

### Available Regions

- **Australia**: Australian news sources
- **United States**: US news outlets
- **United Kingdom**: UK news sources
- **Canada**: Canadian news
- **Singapore**: Singaporean news
- **Taiwan**: Taiwanese news sources
- **Hong Kong**: Hong Kong news
- **Japan**: Japanese news
- **Netherlands**: Dutch news
- **Panama**: Panamanian news

### News Sources Per Region

Each region includes multiple reputable news sources:
- Major newspapers
- News agencies
- Online news outlets
- RSS feeds

## Viewing News

### Selecting a Region

1. Click the region selector dropdown
2. Choose your preferred region
3. News articles load automatically
4. Articles refresh periodically

### News Article Display

Each article shows:
- **Headline**: Article title
- **Description**: Article summary or excerpt
- **Image**: Article thumbnail (if available)
- **Source**: News outlet name
- **Link**: Click to read full article

### Reading Articles

1. Click on any article card
2. Article opens in your default browser
3. Read the full article on the source website
4. Return to DesQTA when done

## News Features

## Troubleshooting

### News Not Loading

**Check:**
- Internet connection
- RSS feed availability
- Region selection

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Try different region
- Check internet connection
- Wait and try again

### Articles Not Opening

**Possible causes:**
- Browser issues
- Link problems
- Source website down

**Solutions:**
- Try different browser
- Check source website directly
- Wait and retry
- Report persistent issues

### Missing Regions

**Note:**
- Not all regions may be available
- Regions depend on RSS feed availability
- Some sources may be temporarily unavailable

**Solutions:**
- Try alternative regions
- Check back later
- Contact support if persistent

### Slow Loading

**Optimize:**
- Check internet speed
- Close other apps
- Refresh the page
- Try different region

## Privacy & Data

### RSS Feeds

- **Public Feeds**: News comes from public RSS feeds
- **No Account**: No login required for news
- **External Links**: Articles open in browser
- **No Tracking**: DesQTA doesn't track news reading

### Source Websites

- **External Sites**: Articles open on source websites
- **Their Policies**: Subject to source website policies
- **Cookies**: Source sites may use cookies
- **Privacy**: Check source website privacy policies
