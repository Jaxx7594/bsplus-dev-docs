## Folios Overview

Folios are digital portfolios that allow you to showcase your work, achievements, and learning progress. They provide a way to organize and present your academic accomplishments over time.

## Accessing Folios

Navigate to Folios from:
- **Sidebar Menu**: Click "Folios" in the navigation sidebar
- **Direct URL**: `/folios`

**Note:** Folios must be enabled by your school administrator. If you don't see the Folios option or get an error message, contact your school's IT department.

## Folios Features

The Folios page provides two main functions:

### Edit My Folios

Create and manage your personal portfolios:
- Build your own folios
- Add content and artifacts
- Organize your work
- Share with teachers or peers

### Browse Folios

Explore and view available folios:
- Browse school-wide folios
- View example portfolios
- See shared folios
- Get inspiration for your own

## Creating a Folio

### Starting a New Folio

1. Click **"Edit My Folios"**
2. Click **"New Folio"** or **"+"** button
3. Enter folio details:
   - **Title**: Name of your folio
   - **Description**: What this folio contains
   - **Subject**: Link to a subject (optional)
   - **Year**: Academic year

### Adding Content

1. Open your folio
2. Click **"Add Item"** or **"+"**
3. Choose content type:
   - **Document**: Upload files
   - **Image**: Add photos or screenshots
   - **Link**: Link to external content
   - **Text**: Write descriptions or reflections

### Organizing Content

- **Drag and Drop**: Reorder items
- **Sections**: Group related items
- **Categories**: Tag and categorize
- **Timeline**: Organize chronologically

## Editing Folios

### Modifying Content

1. Click on any item in your folio
2. Edit details:
   - Change title or description
   - Update content
   - Modify tags or categories
   - Change visibility settings

### Deleting Items

1. Select the item
2. Click delete icon
3. Confirm deletion

**Note:** Deleted items cannot be recovered.

## Sharing Folios

### Sharing Options

- **Private**: Only you can see it
- **Teachers**: Share with your teachers
- **Peers**: Share with classmates
- **Public**: Share with everyone (if enabled)

### Sharing Process

1. Open your folio
2. Click **"Share"** button
3. Select sharing level
4. Add specific recipients (if applicable)
5. Save settings

## Browsing Folios

### Viewing Available Folios

1. Click **"Browse Folios"**
2. Browse by:
   - **Category**: Subject or type
   - **Year**: Academic year
   - **Author**: Who created it
   - **Recent**: Latest folios

### Folio Details

When viewing a folio, you can see:
- **Title and Description**: What it's about
- **Content Items**: All included materials
- **Author Information**: Who created it
- **Date Created**: When it was made
- **Last Updated**: Most recent changes

## Folio Best Practices

### Content Organization

- **Clear Structure**: Organize logically
- **Descriptive Titles**: Make items easy to find
- **Regular Updates**: Keep folios current
- **Quality Over Quantity**: Focus on meaningful work

### Presentation

- **Professional Appearance**: Clean and organized
- **Consistent Formatting**: Uniform style
- **Good Descriptions**: Explain your work
- **Reflection**: Include learning reflections

### Maintenance

- **Regular Review**: Update periodically
- **Remove Outdated**: Keep content relevant
- **Backup Important**: Save copies elsewhere
- **Privacy Check**: Review sharing settings

## Troubleshooting

### Folios Not Available

**Possible causes:**
- Feature not enabled by school
- Account permissions
- School configuration

**Solutions:**
- Contact school administrator
- Check with IT department
- Verify account settings

### Cannot Create Folio

**Check:**
- Folios feature is enabled
- You have creation permissions
- Storage space available

**Solutions:**
- Verify feature is enabled
- Check account permissions
- Free up storage space
- Contact support

### Content Not Loading

**Possible causes:**
- Network issues
- File size too large
- Unsupported file type

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Try smaller files
- Use supported formats
- Refresh the page

### Sharing Not Working

**Check:**
- Sharing permissions
- Recipient access
- Privacy settings

**Solutions:**
- Verify sharing settings
- Check recipient permissions
- Review privacy options
- Contact administrator
